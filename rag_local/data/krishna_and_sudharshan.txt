During the Kurukshetra war, Lord Krishna had vowed not to take up weapons. However, when Bhishma attacked Arjuna fiercely and threatened to kill him, Krishna’s compassion overcame his vow. He picked up a broken chariot wheel and rushed toward Bhishma as if to strike him. Bhishma welcomed this moment, as he wished to see Krishna break his oath for the sake of protecting his devotee. This showed that dharma and protection of righteousness are higher than rigid rules.
